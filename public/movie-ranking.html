The Best Series According to A.B.S

My top 3 series of all-time.

Game of Thrones


An absolute masterpiece of politics and power.
I love the unpredictable plot twists, intense battles, and the deep character development.
The world-building is on another level.

The Blacklist

A perfect mix of crime and intelligence.
Raymond Reddington’s brilliance, global operations, and the strategic mind games make it extremely addictive.

Boston Legal

Smart, witty, and powerful.
The courtroom arguments are sharp and entertaining, and the show balances law, humor, and strong social commentary beautifully.
